Accommodations

  • 25 luxury lodge rooms in our main building. Each room has 1-2 queen-sized beds and a different view of our gardens and grounds.
  • We have one premier suite in the main building with a sitting area, separate bedroom, and small kitchen.
  • Six cabins are available for families or small groups. Each cabin sleeps 6-10 people and has a small kitchen, sitting area, and screened porch.
  • All rooms are non-smoking.
  • Each room and cabin is appointed with books and treasures to make you feel at home.
  • Seasonal rates apply. Call or e-mail us for current rates.
